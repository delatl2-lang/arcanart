Luna the Dark Star

A warrior of the night, cloaked in shadow. Luna Evelyn protects any weary travelers of the night. She envelops the weak and scared within her dark shadows, hiding them away from any danger, whilst she quietly dispatches them with her sword and shield. Striking swiftly from the darkness with ruthlessness and cunning, all travelers who walks underneath the moon's light is safe so long as they are guiltless.
